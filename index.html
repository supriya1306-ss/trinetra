About Trinetra

Trinetra is a community-driven initiative to counter the rapid, uncontrolled spread of disinformation and deepfakes. We combine practical detection, reporting, and digital literacy tools to help users make informed decisions.

  • Hybrid detection: Heuristics now; model-based detection later.
  • Transparency: Show signals and recommendations, not just a score.
  • Privacy-first: Minimal data collection for reports.
